NLP-Driven Consumer Insights | E-commerce Review Analysis

An end-to-end NLP pipeline for turning 22,000+ clothing reviews into interpretable consumer themes.

Overview

This project used natural language processing to extract practical signals from large-scale e-commerce review data. The goal was to surface themes that could support product and retail decisions rather than simply summarize text.

What I built

  • Built an end-to-end NLP pipeline on 22,000+ women's clothing reviews to mine actionable themes from customer language.
  • Applied LDA topic modeling to uncover patterns around fit, quality, and style across the review corpus.
  • Used K-means clustering and visualization to organize themes into usable groups for decision-making and communication.

Why it matters

  • Shows the ability to translate unstructured text into interpretable insights.
  • Connects NLP techniques to a business-facing use case with clear stakeholder value.
  • Demonstrates practical use of modeling, clustering, and visualization in one workflow.
Project details
  • Duration: Jan 2025 – May 2025
  • Associated with: Arizona State University
  • Dataset size: 22,000+ reviews
